Why Verity

Research that's actually trustworthy.

Most AI research tools make things up. Verity is built around a single constraint: every claim must be traceable to something retrieved.

🔍
Live Web Research
Verity searches the real web — not a static database. Every brief reflects what's true today, not six months ago. Sources are cited for every claim.
📊
Confidence Scoring
Every signal is rated high, medium, or low — with a plain-English explanation of why. No black boxes. No inflated certainty on weak data.
💬
Grounded Q&A
Ask any follow-up question. Answers are grounded exclusively in the research retrieved — if the information wasn't found, Verity says so.
🎯
Research Focus Modes
Tailor any brief for a specific purpose — competitive threat assessment, partnership evaluation, investment research, or sales intelligence.
⚡
Real-Time Streaming
Watch the agent work — live. Tool calls, web searches, reasoning, and results stream to the screen as they happen. No black-box waiting.
🔗
Shareable Briefs
Every analysis gets a permanent URL. Share a brief with a colleague, export it as a PDF, or come back to it anytime — all analyses are cached.
